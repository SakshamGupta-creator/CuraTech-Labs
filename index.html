  • Blood Sugar Tracking

Who is GlucoEase for?

GlucoEase is suitable for anyone who needs to monitor their blood glucose levels on a regular basis. This includes people with Type 1, Type 2 and rarer forms of Diabetes such as Type 3c and Latent Autoimmune Diabetes in Adults (LADA).

GlucoEase will be suitable for people with gestational Diabetes and we expect it to be useful for sports people and anyone interested in health and nutrition.

Key features of GlucoEase

  • Totally non-invasive and pain free
  • Real-time results (no lag)
  • Continuous detection
  • Transform your current watch and smart device into a glucose monitor
  • Alarm alerts, trend profiles and time-in range profile
  • Integrated motion detection and environmental sensors
  • Bluetooth connectivity to smart device
  • iOS and Android compatible stand-alone sensor
  • User interface via app on smart device
  • No additional running cost
  • No risk of it falling off and needing replacement

Frequently Asked Questions

What is blood glucose monitoring?

Blood glucose monitoring is when a person tracks their blood glucose levels to identify information on when they go up and down.

What is a continuous glucose monitor or CGM?

A CGM is a small sensor device attached to the skin that measures how much glucose is in the interstitial fluid under your skin.

Why is it important to monitor blood glucose continuously?

People with Diabetes need to measure their blood glucose continuously so they can take action if the levels go too high or too low.

Where will I be able to buy GlucoEase

GlucoEase will be available online. We will release more specific information on where you will be able to purchase once the device is ready for market.

Can you set alerts on GlucoEase

Yes, there will be an alert function allowing the user to set thresholds so that they are alerted when their blood glucose levels go into either the hyperglycaemic or hypoglycaemic zone.

Will it sync with a smartphone app?

GlucoEase will have a companion app that can be downloaded to both iOS and android smartphone device.

Will GlucoEase be needleless?

The GlucoEase device will not contain a needle and will not penetrate the skin.

How does GlucoEase work?

GlucoEase works by using near-infrared technology(ranging from 900 nm to 1700 nm) to read the levels of glucose in the blood.
